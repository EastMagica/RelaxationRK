# Lotka–Volterra Equations

The Lotka–Volterra Equations

$$
\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}t}\begin{pmatrix}
u_1(t) \\
u_2(t) \\
\end{pmatrix}=
\begin{pmatrix}
u_1(t)(1 - u_2(t)) \\
u_2(t)(u_1(t) - 1) \\
\end{pmatrix},\quad
u(0)=\begin{pmatrix}
1 \\ 2
\end{pmatrix} \tag{1}
$$

$$
H(u)=u_1 - \log(u_1) + u_2 - \log(u_2) \tag{2}
$$
